Language Learning App

ShotLingua

A language learning app that helps you speak everyday topics in another language faster

ShotLingua is a language learning app designed to help you use Korean, English, Japanese, and Chinese in real life, at work, and in meaningful relationships. It supports people who want to explain work manuals in another language or guide customers more clearly while working part-time or in service jobs. When studying abroad or living overseas, you can take photos of transportation signs and local information to learn useful vocabulary and expressions for that exact situation. You can also take pictures of menus and shop information to learn how to order, explain things to customers, and practice practical conversation through audio content and speaking exercises. ShotLingua also aims to help you talk about the same topics you already discuss with friends and family in your native language, but in another language much sooner. Based on your social media chat history and the kinds of things you discuss with AI tools you use every day, it helps organize the vocabulary and expressions you actually need so you can explain what you are talking about now in another language. Rather than just memorizing words, it helps you express your thoughts and feelings more naturally and build deeper relationships with people from other countries.

Main Features

Designed to make language learning easier to continue by connecting it with daily conversation, study abroad, travel, and work situations.

Situation-based vocabulary learning

Learn vocabulary and expressions related to transportation, signs, menus, and work manuals so you can understand what you need in real situations.

Camera translation and photo-based learning

Take photos of text you encounter and turn them directly into learning opportunities, especially useful while traveling or living abroad.

Conversation practice and audio learning

Practice ordering food, helping customers, and handling daily conversations through practical speaking and listening exercises.

Vocabulary tailored to your life

Organize the vocabulary and expressions you really need based on your chat history and everyday AI conversations, so you can talk about your real life sooner.
